Pecan Pie
Serving size: 8 people

Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2cup white sugar
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, melted
2 eggs
1 tbsp flour
1 tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup pecan halves
1 9-inch unbaked pie crust

Recipe:
Preheat the oven to 400F.
Whisk the eggs in a large bowl. Add melted butter and mix until combined. 
Add brown sugar, white sugar and flour and mix until combined. 
Add the milk, vanilla and pecans and mix until combined.
Pour the pecan mixture into a pie crust.
Put the pecan pie in the oven and bake for 40 minutes.
